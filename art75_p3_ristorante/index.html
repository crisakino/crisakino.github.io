ABOUT CATFISH

Far enough off the beaten path to feel like a catch—but not far enough to lose business—'Catfish Cafe' was founded on the principle of adventurous gourmet life! Never a dull moment in dining or experience, this cafe allows customers serendipitous enough to find it the chance to experience one man's strange-love for all things 90's anime to kung-fu movies and back!

In addition to our full espresso and brew bar menu, we serve a rotating menu of fresh and creative familiar world-dishes with a unique take—as well as a selection of sides and salads among other things... if you're feeling lucky.

"You should enjoy the little detours to the fullest. Because that's where you'll find the things more important than what you want."

Chef, Coffeeist and Owner: Ging Freecss

Opening hours: everyday from 6am to 5pm.
