ABOUT VCASSS

Virtual Cafe Action was originally a VR game which placed the player in an environment where they could learn the basics of food service and preparation—all while experiencing the good (and bad) of customer service, with a full cast of customers and coworkers to interact. In a candid interview, the creator mentioned his true purpose of the game was never to train, but to entertain. He wanted his audience to enjoy the restaurant he never had time to open. Shibuya//Streetwise//Stop is an unofficial homage to that vision, dedicated to emulating the game's original cafe environment to a tee, so that the virtual action may finally become a reality.

GAME DESCRIPTION:VCA: Virtual Cafe Action is a VR style Visual Novel about Kung-fu movies, the existential horror of salaryman life, the deep intricacies of Neon Genesis Evangelion and Post-dsytopian stoicism. Oh, and coffee.

Although VCASSS—now referred to as 'Vacay's'—has carved out its own identity in recent years, fans from all over the world still travel to the quaint midtown storefront to experience the surprising faithfullness to character the little cafe holds.

In addition to our full espresso and brew bar menu, we serve a rotating menu of refreshing and familiar home-dishes from around the world as well as a selection of sides and salads among other treats. All menu options are indeed in-game items; If you ask your waiter, they will gladly geek out about it.

"You should enjoy the little detours to the fullest. Because that's where you'll find the things more important than what you want."
